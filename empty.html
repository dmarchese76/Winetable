The Wine consumption in the U.S. has been declining over the last decade.

Except for 2020, when it increased from 2.19 gals per resident in 2019 to 3.12 gals in 2020, a trend that conitued during 2021 reaching a 3.16 gals per resident, before dropping again to 2.96 in 2022.
